**Overseas** is an educational website developed in 2024 as part of a project for the High School English subject. The main goal of the platform is to assist users who wish to travel or live in other countries, offering a robust data comparator and detailed information about various global destinations.

---

### Key Features

- **Country Comparator:** A tool that allows side-by-side visualization of statistics and important data between different nations.
- **Immigration Data:** Crucial information about visas, the job market, housing, and health systems for those planning to reside abroad.
- **Travel Guide:** Essential tips and data for tourists, facilitating itinerary planning and local culture understanding.
- **Modern Interface:** User experience-focused design, with intuitive navigation and high-quality interactive components.
- **Fullstack Architecture:** Complete integration between a dynamic frontend and a scalable backend for geographic information management.

### Technical and Design Challenges

As a project developed within the **High School** context, the focus was to demonstrate how technology can be applied to solve complex informational problems, such as internationalization research.

1. **Data Consolidation:** The biggest challenge was gathering and structuring diverse data from multiple countries into a coherent database schema using Sequelize, ensuring accurate comparisons.
2. **Animations and Feedback:** The implementation of Framer Motion allowed for smooth transitions that make navigating through dense data much friendlier and less tiring for the user.
3. **Code Organization:** Choosing a TypeScript environment on both ends (Client and Server) ensured greater data traffic security and facilitated long-term project maintenance.
