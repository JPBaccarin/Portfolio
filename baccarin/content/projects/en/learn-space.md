> _Capstone Project (TCC) ‚Äî 2024_

**LearnSpace** is a complete educational platform that uses generative AI to personalize learning. The system was designed to serve both students and teachers, offering everything from automated essay grading to real-time English pronunciation training.

---

### üöÄ Key Features

- **AI for Essay Correction:** Instant analysis based on official criteria using the OpenAI API.
- **English Conversation:** Training for pronunciation and fluency with AI feedback and voice synthesis.
- **Automated Study Plans:** Schedules generated dynamically according to the student's objective.
- **Profile Management (RBAC):** Distinct permission system for Teachers and Students, with support for Free and Premium plans.
- **Study Ecosystem:** Bank of entrance exam questions, progress Dashboards, interactive Periodic Table, and article Blog.

### üí° Technical Challenges & Leadership

As **Project Lead**, I managed a team of 5 people, being responsible for System Design, Database modeling, and Full-stack architecture. The main challenges overcome were:

1. **Performance Optimization:** I reduced the initial loading time from **2 minutes to just 40 seconds**, applying Server-Side Rendering (SSR) techniques and optimizing native Next.js images and fonts.
2. **API Cost Management:** I implemented caching strategies and prompt refinement to minimize the consumption of OpenAI and ElevenLabs APIs, ensuring the project's financial viability.
3. **Team Leadership:** Coordination of sprints, code review, and alignment between the design and development areas to ensure delivery within the deadline.
